RebelLUG LEGO® Instructions Store

RebelLUG members often get requests for instructions to their custom LEGO® models. We're happy to announce that those are finally available here on the RebelLUG website.

The ordering process is simple. Through the "quick buy" button, you can find more information about each model, including more pictures, videos, and a complete parts list. To purchase a set of instructions, you can use any of the PayPal options near the bottom of each models' page. Immediately after placing your order, you will recieve a PDF copy of the instructions to the email linked with your PayPal. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us via email at rebellegousergroup@gmail.com.

Thank you for helping support our builders and our group!
