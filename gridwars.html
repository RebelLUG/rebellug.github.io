GRID Wars: Episode IV: A New Contest

The Challenge

  • Build a custom build of a Star Wars Vehicle, using the Tron Aesthetic (Black with bright neon colours)
  • Any Vehicle from the Star Wars universe is allowed - Movie, TV, Comics, Books, or even Extended Universe. Please include a reference photo if your chosen vehicle is obscure.
  • Build must be new, built after Oct 15, 2018
  • No size limit on your builds
  • Builds can be any scale - Minifig / Micro / 1:1
  • Brick built backgrounds are not required

The Rules

  • Submit one (1) photo to this GRID WARS Flickr Group
  • Please use #GridWarsIV hastag in other social posts
  • No digital entries, only LEGO elements can be used, however third party blasters/minifigs (CAC, EclipseGrafix etc) are allowed
  • You may create as many entries as you like, but you may only place once
  • Submit your build before Nov 15, 2018. This is the contest's deadline!

The Prizes

    • 75205 - Mos Eisley Cantina™
    • 21314 TRON: Legacy
    • Participation Brick / Trophy
  • Second Place
    • 75200 Ahch-To Island™ Training
    • 21314 TRON: Legacy
    • Participation Brick / Trophy
  • Third Place
    • 41627 Luke Skywalker™ & Yoda™
    • 21314 TRON: Legacy
    • Participation Brick / Trophy

Participants attending any RebelLUG Events will also receive Participation bricks (while supplies last). Thank you to eclipseGRAFX for sponsoring this contest.
