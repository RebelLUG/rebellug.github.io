Assemble The Fleet Contest

Phase 1: The Contest

  • Build a micro scale Capital or Frigate ship
  • Build a micro fighter or bomber squad
  • Ships can be of your own design or from Star Wars, but should fit in the Star Wars universe
  • Build must be new, built after Feb 2, 2020
  • The Capital or Frigate ship must be less than 32 studs
  • Squads can be a lonely fighter or up to squad of 5

The Rules

  • Submit one (1) photo of your Capital or Frigate ship to the contest Flickr Group
  • Submit one (1) photo of your Fighter or Bomber squad to the contest Flickr Group
  • If you do not have a Flickr account, you can just use the hashtag #AssembleTheFleet2020 and I'll post a link to your entry for you.
  • No digital entries, only LEGO elements can be used, however third party prints and pieces (CAC, EclipseGrafix etc) are allowed
  • You may create as many entries as you like ,but you must have defined pair of Capital or Frigate ship and fighter or bomber squad. You may only only place once in contest.
  • Submit your build before Feb 29, 2020. This is the contest's deadline!

Phase 2: The Convention Collaboration

  • Join the RebelLUG Resistance fleet!
  • For bonus swag, display your builds with the rest of the fleet, and face off against the Final Order Star Destroyers
  • Rendezvous coordinates:
  • Brickworld Chicago - June 17 – 21, 2020
  • Bricks in The Six Toronto - July 23– 26, 2020
  • Brickfair Virginia - July 29 - Aug 2, 2020
