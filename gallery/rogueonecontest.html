Rogue One Contest

The Challenge

  • Build your favorite scene from Rogue One
  • May Use 3rd party figures/accessories (brickarms or CAC) but all other pieces must be LEGO
  • No size requirements

The Rules

  • Contest closes February 1st, 2017
  • Submit entry by uploading a video to YouTube and email the link to: rebellegousergroup@gmail.com
  • MOCs will be judged based on detail, not size
  • RebelLUG members will vote on winner

Contest restults:

  • Winner: The Brick Puke
    • Prize:
    • 2x 75164 LEGO Star Wars Rebel Trooper Battle Pack
    • 18x CAC Series 3 Weapons

About

RebelLUG is a private, online LEGO® User Group made up of independent creators from YouTube, Instagram, and Flickr working together on custom LEGO® projects.
