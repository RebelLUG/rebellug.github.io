Attack on Ryloth

Brickworld 2016 was the first time RebelLUG met in person and displayed together at a convention under the RebelLUG name and banner. Our collaboration that year was called "Attack on Ryloth," a scene depicted the Empire trying to take over a town on Ryloth and some Rebel soldiers trying to defend it. It was nominated for Best Group Layout. It was also sponsored by Arealight, who gave us the custom Twi'lek heads. Lego Buff Productions, Kris Productions, LegoSpencer, alcloneproductions, Daniel Barwegen, and LegoLord82 worked on this project.
