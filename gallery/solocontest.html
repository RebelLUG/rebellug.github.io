Solo Contest

The Challenge

  • Build a custom recreation of a scene from Solo: A Star Wars Story
  • Build at your preferred scale (minifig-scale or micro-scale)
  • Build larger than 16x16 studs
  • Complete and post before July 14, 2018 at 11:59pm PST
  • You will be judged on creativity and attention to detail

The Rules

  • Post either three pictures of your MOC to your Instagram with the hashtag #rebellugsolo or post a YouTube video of your MOC
  • Submit links to either the Instagram pictures or the YouTube video in the form found at solo.rebellug.com
  • Your MOC must be uploaded after the beginning of the contest (no old MOCs)
  • Use only LEGO elements, however third party-custom blasters or minifigures (CAC, EclipseGrafx) are allowed
  • There is no limitation on location, the contest is international
  • You may enter as many times as you like, but only one MOC will win

Contest restults:

  • First Place: Brick Ninja
    • Prize:
    • 75212 LEGO Star Wars Kessel Run Millenium Falcon

About
