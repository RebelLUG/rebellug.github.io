    First Order Lego

    Social Media Manager

    Luke overlooks all media posted on every platform to ensure a clear and coherent message is being presented to the community.

    "Hi there! My name is Luke I'm an Australian LEGO builder that currently lives in Croatia (Europe). I am 18 years old and have been building MOCs from the age of 9. My favorite theme to build in is definitely Star Wars especially from the Battlefront video games. But I occasionally build Witcher, World War or Lord Of The Rings MOCs. Detail and general "coolness" is what comes first when I'm building. I also love hiding easter eggs in my MOCs so keep an eye out. ;)​"

    Norlego

    Secretary

    Olav is the bookkeeper for the LUG. He monitors participation by members and manages member perk programs.

    "Hey, my name is Olav, and I’m an 18-year-old LEGO builder from Norway. I got back into LEGO building around early 2016 and since then I’ve been very active on social media platforms such as Instagram, Flickr and Youtube. I love sharing pictures of my creations not only when they’re completed, but also during the building process. My field of expertise has grown to become Lord of the Rings and Star Wars, but I also enjoy building MOCs based on themes like Pirates and Castle."
