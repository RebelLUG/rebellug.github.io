LEGO Halo Warthog

By Nick Jensen - Nick Brick

❮ ❯

The Warthog is a light recon vehicle equipped to fight alien invaders of Earth and her colonies. This model features rolling wheels, seats for two minifigures and one standing in turret, and a rotating machine gun turret. It has the durability to hold together well for light play. The suspension struts may snap with heavy pressure. It does not steer. It has 349 pieces.
