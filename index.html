About RebelLUG

We are a private, online LEGO® User Group made up of independent creators from YouTube, Instagram, and Flickr working together on custom LEGO® projects. We were founded in 2015 as something to call our group of seven friends so we could work on big projects together, under a single name. Since then we’ve grown to 37 members across 11 countries. We’ve been able to work together on many different custom LEGO MOC collaborations both online and at conventions since then. We strive to build LEGO to the best of our ability, in new creative ways, and encourage other members of the online LEGO community to do the same.
